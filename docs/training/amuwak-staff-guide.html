Welcome & the basics

Five minutes that make everything else easier.

The Amuwak Staff app is your tool in the field. You use it to pick up laundry from customers, price it, track it through the shop, and hand it back — all from your phone.

Who this app is for

This is a staff & rider tool, not the customer's app. You operate it on behalf of the customer — you enter their details, take the photos, and confirm pickups and deliveries with them in person.

📦 Pickups
Create an order, weigh and photograph the laundry, and tag the bag.
💵 Pricing
The app prices each order automatically from weight × rate, plus any special items.
🚪 Deliveries
Photograph the hand-over and mark the order complete.
💡 The golden ruleAlways confirm with the customer before you tap the final button — the item count, the bag tag at pickup, and the hand-over at delivery. The photos you take are the shop's proof.

Before you start your shift

  • Make sure your phone has internet — the app needs a connection to load and save orders.
  • Allow Camera and Location permissions when asked (you'll need them for photos and addresses).
  • Have your username and PIN ready (your supervisor sets these up).

Signing in

Start of every shift.
  1. Open the app. You'll see the Amuwak Staff login screen.
  2. Type your Username.
  3. Type your PIN (it stays hidden as you type).
  4. Tap Login. The button shows a spinner while it checks.

If a field is empty you'll see Enter your username or Enter your PIN. If the username or PIN is wrong, a red message appears under the PIN box — check and try again.

⚠️ Keep your PIN privateNever share your PIN. At the end of your shift, sign out from the Account tab (Section 10) so nobody else can use your login.

Step 1 — Create a new pickup

From Home → New pickup.
  1. Tap New pickup on the Home tab.
  2. Enter the Customer name and Phone (a 9-digit Ugandan number).
  3. For the address, type it or tap 📍 Use my location to fill it in automatically.
  4. If the phone matches someone you've served before, a sheet pops up: “Existing customer found.” Tap Use this customer to reuse their details (and their agreed rate), or Different customer.
  5. Pick the Service type: Wash & Iron, Dry cleaning, Iron only, or Wash only.
  6. Choose Pickup now or Schedule for later (then a chip like In 1 hour or Tomorrow morning).
  7. The Rate: USh …/kg line shows the price per kg that will apply.
  8. (Optional) Tap Add optional details for item count, notes, or a Custom rate for this customer.
  9. Tap Create pickup.

Step 2 — Confirm the pickup & tag the bag

Opens after you create a pickup, or via Confirm pickup on the order.

This is the “Confirm pickup” screen. Do it with the customer present.

  1. Set How many items? using the − / + buttons.
  2. Take Photos (up to 3) — tap Add photo. At least one photo is required.
  3. Enter the Estimated weight (kg).
  4. Add any Special items (e.g. a blanket priced separately).
  5. Check the total — it shows a “Provisional” tag until the final weight is set later.
  6. Tap Confirm with customer.
  7. On the “Tag the bag” screen, write the order number on the bag (or attach the QR), then tap Done.
💡 Good photos protect everyoneCapture the laundry clearly and include anything already stained or damaged. This is the shop's record of what was collected.

Pricing made simple

The app does the maths — here's how it works.

Every order's total is worked out the same way:

Weight × Rate per kg+Special items+Adjustment
The total is never less than USh 0. Amounts show in shillings, e.g. USh 15,000.

Provisional vs final

At pickup you enter an estimated weight, so the total shows a “Provisional” tag. When the laundry is finished you set the final weight on the order — the tag disappears and the total is locked in.

Which rate is used?

The app picks the rate in this order:

  1. A custom rate you typed for this order, if any.
  2. The customer's saved rate, if they have one.
  3. The shop's default rate.
💡 Frozen at creationEach order remembers the rate it was created with. Changing the shop default later does not re-price old orders.

Editing pricing on an order

Open the order → Pricing section. You can set:

  • Final weight (kg)
  • Special items (add/remove)
  • Manual adjustment (UGX, +/−) — a discount (negative) or surcharge (positive)

Then tap Save pricing. You'll see “Pricing saved.”

Setting the default rate

Go to Account → Pricing settings, enter the Default rate per kg (UGX), and tap Save. This is the rate used for new customers who don't have their own.

⚠️ Handle the default with careThe default rate affects every new order for customers without a custom rate. Only change it when you're sure.

Managing an order

Tap any order card to open Order details.

The Order details screen shows everything about one order:

  • A status chip (the order's current stage).
  • Customer — name, phone, address.
  • Laundry details — order code, service, items, time.
  • Pricing — rate, weights, special items, adjustment, total, Save pricing.
  • Notes and History (a log of what happened and when).

Step 3 — Hand over (delivery)

When the order is Ready for delivery, tap Deliver.

This opens the “Hand over” screen.

  1. Check the From pickup card — it reminds you of the item count and how many pickup photos are on file.
  2. Take Handover photos (up to 3) as the customer receives the laundry. At least one is required.
  3. Add Notes if needed.
  4. Tap Mark delivered. The order moves to Completed. 🎉
💡 Close the loop in personPhotograph the hand-over while you're with the customer. It confirms the laundry was returned.

Everyday tools

Find orders, stay informed, and check your day.

🔎 Search & scan

From Home tap Check order. Search by code, name, phone, or address in the box, or tap the scan icon to read a bag's QR tag. If nothing matches you'll see “No orders found.”

🔔 Notifications

The bell (top-right of the dashboard) lists recent activity — New pickups waiting and orders Delivered in the last 48h — with the order code, customer, status and time. The badge number tells you how many pickups need attention.

📊 Daily report

The Report tab summarises your day: Orders, Items, Completed, and Pending work, plus a Status breakdown bar for each stage. At the bottom: “Total items handled today.”

👤 Account & sign out

The Account tab shows your staff profile, a shortcut to Pricing settings, and the Sign out button. Signing out asks “Sign out of this device?” — tap Sign out to confirm. Always sign out at the end of your shift.

When something goes wrong

Common messages and what to do.
You see…What it means & what to do
Could not load orders. Please try again.The app couldn't reach the server. Check your internet, then tap Retry. If it persists, tell your supervisor.
Could not reserve an order number…Connection blip while creating the order. Check signal and tap Create pickup again — it won't create a duplicate.
Customer was saved, but the order could not be saved.The customer saved but the order didn't. Tap Create pickup again to finish it.
Camera permission denied…Enable Camera for the app in your phone's Settings, then take the photo again.
Could not determine address…Location lookup failed — just type the address by hand.
Pickup confirmed, but pricing wasn't saved…Pickup is done, but weight/items didn't save. Open the order and add them in the Pricing section.
Session expired — please sign in again.You've been signed out for safety. Log in again to continue.
Pricing settings missing — contact admin.The shop's default rate isn't set up. Tell your supervisor/admin.
💡 When in doubtMost problems are connection-related. Move to better signal and retry — the app is built to let you tap the same button again safely without creating duplicates.

Coming soon

What we're planning to improve. These are not yet in the app.

We're actively improving Amuwak. Here's what's on the roadmap so you know what to expect:

🔜 Work offline, sync laterToday the app needs internet to load and save. We're bringing back offline mode so you can create pickups and capture proof with no signal — and the app syncs automatically once you're back online.
🔜 Role-based permissionsRight now any signed-in staff member can change the shop's default rate. We plan to restrict pricing settings to managers only.
🔜 A dedicated intake screenFinal weight is currently entered on the Order details screen. A purpose-built intake step will make weighing finished laundry faster and clearer.
🔜 Manage customer rates anytimeA per-customer rate can currently only be set while creating a pickup. We're adding a place to view and edit a customer's agreed rate at any time.
🔜 Live updates & richer reportsLists will refresh instantly when anything changes, and the daily report will grow into fuller history and analytics across days and staff.

Have an idea or hit a snag? Tell your supervisor — field feedback shapes what we build next.
